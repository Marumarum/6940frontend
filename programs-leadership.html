Leadership Training

Performance reviews. Hiring people. Giving feedback. ​Leadership can be quite a challenge.

Too often people are put into leadership positions without having the proper training. Recognizing that this can be a problem Northeastern has created a leadership development path to help those with little to no experience managing others all the way to people at the most senior levels of the organization. Review the path below and click on an appropriate area to learn more and begin your journey.

Courses are divided into five steps:

  • Step 1: New to being a manager at Northeastern?
  • Step 2: Take courses designed specifically for managers.
  • Step 3: Nominate yourself to be part of a cohort of leaders.
  • Step 4: A by nomination cohort for managers with 5 to 10 years of experience.
  • Step 5: A by nomination cohort for senior executives at Northeastern.
